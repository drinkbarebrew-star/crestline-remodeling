Custom Cabinetry

North Shore Custom Cabinetry You Can Count On

Stock cabinets compromise on fit, finish, and storage. Crestline Remodeling designs and installs custom and semi-custom cabinetry that uses every inch — built around your ceiling heights, appliance specs, and the way you actually cook and store.

Working with regional millwork shops, we deliver furniture-grade kitchen cabinetry, bathroom vanities, mudroom lockers, libraries, and built-in entertainment walls that fit North Shore homes precisely and last for decades.

Quick stat: 100% — built to your exact dimensions.

What’s Included

  • Custom and semi-custom kitchen cabinetry
  • Bathroom vanities and linen storage
  • Library, office, and built-in bookcases
  • Mudroom lockers and entryway storage systems
  • Entertainment centers and fireplace surrounds
  • Paint-grade and stain-grade finish options

What Does Custom Cabinetry Cost in Highland Park?

A full kitchen’s custom cabinetry typically runs $25,000–$60,000; individual built-ins commonly run $4,000–$15,000 each. Every project is different, so Crestline provides a fixed-price proposal after the design phase — no vague ranges, no surprise change orders.

Typical investment: $12,000 – $75,000 for custom cabinetry on the North Shore.

Our Process

  1. Free consultation — we visit your home, listen to your goals, and discuss a realistic budget range.
  2. Design & engineering — floor plans, 3D renderings, and material selections with real-time pricing.
  3. Fixed-price proposal — a detailed, line-item contract you approve before any work begins.
  4. Construction — a dedicated project manager, clean job sites, and weekly progress updates.
  5. Final walkthrough & warranty — we don’t finish until you’re thrilled, and we stand behind our work.

Custom Cabinetry FAQs

Common questions about custom cabinetry on Chicago’s North Shore.

Custom cabinetry in Highland Park typically runs $12,000 to $75,000+ depending on scope. A full kitchen’s cabinetry usually runs $25,000–$60,000, while individual built-ins like a home office or entertainment wall run $4,000–$15,000 each. Custom work costs more than stock but eliminates wasted space and fits your home exactly.
Semi-custom cabinetry starts from standard sizes with modifications to dimensions, finishes, and storage features — a great value for many projects. Fully custom cabinetry is built from scratch to any dimension, finish, and configuration, ideal for unusual spaces, tall ceilings, and furniture-grade built-ins. We’ll recommend the right level for your goals and budget.
Custom cabinetry typically takes 6 to 12 weeks from final design approval to delivery, depending on the shop’s queue and the complexity of your order. We factor this lead time into the overall project schedule so cabinetry arrives exactly when the space is ready, avoiding storage and damage risk.
