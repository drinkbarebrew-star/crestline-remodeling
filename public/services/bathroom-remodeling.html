Why Invest

Why Bathroom Remodeling?

Bathroom remodeling consistently ranks among the top home improvement projects for return on investment. A mid-range bathroom remodel in Chicago typically costs between $25,000 and $50,000, with higher-end projects reaching $75,000 or more. The investment pays off both in daily enjoyment and at resale -- updated bathrooms are among the first things buyers look for.
