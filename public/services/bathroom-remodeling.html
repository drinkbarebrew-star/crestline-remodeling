Bathroom Remodeling

North Shore Bathroom Remodeling You Can Count On

From compact powder rooms to expansive primary suites, Crestline Remodeling transforms North Shore bathrooms into private retreats. We specialize in the waterproofing, ventilation, and substrate detailing that older Highland Park homes demand — the work behind the tile that determines whether a bathroom lasts 5 years or 25.

Our crews have completed over 450 bathroom remodels, including curbless walk-in showers, heated floors, and steam systems that hold up to the realities of Midwest seasons.

Quick stat: 450+ — bathrooms remodeled.

What’s Included

  • Custom walk-in, curbless, and frameless glass showers
  • Full waterproofing systems (Schluter / liquid membrane)
  • Freestanding soaking tubs and integrated tub-shower combinations
  • Custom vanities, double-bowl configurations, and stone tops
  • Heated tile flooring and towel warming systems
  • Upgraded ventilation, lighting, and humidity control

What Does Bathroom Remodeling Cost in Highland Park?

A guest or hall bath typically runs $18,000–$35,000; a full primary suite remodel on the North Shore commonly runs $40,000–$65,000+. Every project is different, so Crestline provides a fixed-price proposal after the design phase — no vague ranges, no surprise change orders.

Typical investment: $18,000 – $65,000 for bathroom remodeling on the North Shore.

Our Process

  1. Free consultation — we visit your home, listen to your goals, and discuss a realistic budget range.
  2. Design & engineering — floor plans, 3D renderings, and material selections with real-time pricing.
  3. Fixed-price proposal — a detailed, line-item contract you approve before any work begins.
  4. Construction — a dedicated project manager, clean job sites, and weekly progress updates.
  5. Final walkthrough & warranty — we don’t finish until you’re thrilled, and we stand behind our work.

Bathroom Remodeling FAQs

Common questions about bathroom remodeling on Chicago’s North Shore.

Bathroom remodels in Highland Park range from about $18,000 for a guest bath refresh to $65,000+ for a full primary suite with a custom shower, heated floors, and double vanity. Tile selection, glass enclosures, and moving plumbing fixtures are the main cost variables. Crestline gives you a fixed-price proposal before any demolition.
Most bathroom remodels take 4 to 7 weeks on site. A straightforward guest bath can be done in 3 to 4 weeks, while a primary suite with custom tile, a steam shower, and structural changes may take 7 to 9 weeks. Waterproofing and tile work cannot be rushed without risking failures down the line.
