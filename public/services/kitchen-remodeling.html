North Shore Kitchen Remodeling You Can Count On

Your kitchen is the hardest-working room in the house, and on the North Shore it’s also the room guests gravitate to. Crestline Remodeling designs and builds kitchens that balance everyday function with the timeless, gallery-quality finishes Highland Park homeowners expect.

Since 2009, our design-build team has completed more than 600 kitchen projects across Chicago’s North Shore — from compact galley updates to full structural reconfigurations that open the kitchen to the family room.

Quick stat: 600+ — kitchens completed since 2009.

What’s Included

  • Custom and semi-custom cabinetry from regional millwork partners
  • Quartz, granite, and natural stone countertop fabrication
  • Structural wall removal and load-bearing beam engineering
  • Professional-grade appliance integration and panel-ready fronts
  • Hardwood, tile, and large-format porcelain flooring
  • Recessed, pendant, and under-cabinet lighting design

What Does Kitchen Remodeling Cost in Highland Park?

A mid-range Highland Park kitchen remodel typically lands between $65,000 and $95,000; high-end projects with custom cabinetry and structural changes can exceed $150,000. Every project is different, so Crestline provides a fixed-price proposal after the design phase — no vague ranges, no surprise change orders.

Typical investment: $45,000 – $120,000 for kitchen remodeling on the North Shore.

Our Process

  1. Free consultation — we visit your home, listen to your goals, and discuss a realistic budget range.
  2. Design & engineering — floor plans, 3D renderings, and material selections with real-time pricing.
  3. Fixed-price proposal — a detailed, line-item contract you approve before any work begins.
  4. Construction — a dedicated project manager, clean job sites, and weekly progress updates.
  5. Final walkthrough & warranty — we don’t finish until you’re thrilled, and we stand behind our work.

Kitchen Remodeling FAQs

Common questions about kitchen remodeling on Chicago’s North Shore.

Most full kitchen remodels in Highland Park range from $45,000 to $120,000, with the majority of our projects landing between $65,000 and $95,000. The biggest cost drivers are cabinetry (typically 30–35% of the budget), countertops, and whether you’re moving walls or plumbing. Crestline provides a fixed-price proposal after the design phase so there are no mid-project surprises.
A typical North Shore kitchen remodel takes 8 to 12 weeks of on-site construction, preceded by 4 to 6 weeks of design and material selection. Projects involving structural changes, custom cabinetry lead times, or permit-heavy scopes can run 14 to 16 weeks. We provide a week-by-week schedule before demolition begins.
Yes — any kitchen remodel involving electrical, plumbing, or structural work requires a permit from the City of Highland Park or your local municipality. As a licensed and insured design-build firm, Crestline handles all permit applications, inspections, and code compliance on your behalf.
