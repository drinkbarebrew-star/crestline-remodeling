Why Whole-Home Renovation?

A whole-home renovation allows you to address every aspect of your home in a coordinated effort, often saving 15-20% compared to tackling projects individually over time. It is also the most effective way to bring a dated home up to current standards for energy efficiency, safety, and livability while preserving the character and location you love.

  • Free in-home consultation and design
  • Fixed-price contracts with no surprises
  • Dedicated project manager throughout
  • 5-year workmanship warranty
