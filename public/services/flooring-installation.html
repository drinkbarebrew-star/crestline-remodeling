Flooring Installation Services in Chicago

Hardwood, tile, luxury vinyl, and natural stone flooring.

Get a Free Flooring Installation Estimate

Beautiful floors set the foundation for every room in your home. Crestline Remodeling installs hardwood, tile, luxury vinyl plank, natural stone, and carpet with expert precision and lasting quality. Whether you are updating a single room or installing new floors throughout your entire home, our team delivers flawless results on time and within budget.

Hardwood Flooring

Solid and engineered hardwood in oak, walnut, maple, hickory, and exotic species. Site-finished or pre-finished to your preference.

Porcelain & Ceramic Tile

Floor tile installations for kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, and mudrooms with precision leveling and grouting.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

Waterproof, durable, and realistic wood-look planks ideal for basements, kitchens, and high-traffic areas.

Natural Stone

Marble, travertine, slate, and limestone floors installed with proper substrate preparation and sealing for lasting beauty.

Heated Floor Systems

In-floor radiant heating installed beneath tile or stone for luxurious warmth in bathrooms and entryways.

Floor Refinishing

Sanding, staining, and refinishing existing hardwood floors to restore their original beauty or change their color.

Why Invest

Why Flooring Installation?

New flooring is one of the most transformative and cost-effective home improvements. In the Chicago market, quality flooring installation typically costs $6 to $25 per square foot for materials and labor, depending on the material selected. Updated flooring improves daily comfort, enhances the visual flow between rooms, and significantly boosts resale value.
