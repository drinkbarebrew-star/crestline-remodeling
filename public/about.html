About Crestline Remodeling

Building trust, one home at a time.

Our Story

Craftsmanship Meets Integrity

Crestline Remodeling was founded with a simple belief: homeowners deserve a remodeling experience built on honesty, clear communication, and exceptional craftsmanship. What started as a small team of skilled tradespeople serving Chicago's North Shore has grown into one of the region's most trusted home remodeling firms.

Over more than a decade, we have completed over 500 projects across the Chicagoland area -- from intimate bathroom refreshes in Wilmette to full-scale home renovations in Highland Park and Hinsdale. Every project, regardless of size, receives the same level of care, attention, and professionalism.

Our team includes licensed general contractors, master carpenters, certified tile installers, and dedicated project managers. We handle design, permitting, construction, and final punch-list with a single point of accountability. No subcontractor runaround. No surprise invoices. Just quality work delivered on schedule.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Dedicated project manager for every job

Our Process

Every Crestline project follows a proven four-step process designed for transparency and results.

01

Consultation

We visit your home, listen to your vision, assess the space, and discuss budget, timeline, and design preferences. No pressure, no obligation.

02

Design & Planning

Our team develops a detailed scope of work, material selections, and a fixed-price proposal. You approve every detail before we begin.

03

Construction

Your dedicated project manager oversees every phase -- demolition, rough-in, installation, and finishing. Weekly updates keep you informed.

04

Final Walkthrough

We walk every detail together, complete any punch-list items, and ensure your total satisfaction before the project is officially complete.
