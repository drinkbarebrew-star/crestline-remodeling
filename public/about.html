About Crestline Remodeling

The North Shore’s design-build firm, built on craftsmanship and accountability since 2009.

Our Story

Built on the North Shore, for the North Shore

Crestline Remodeling was founded in 2009 in Highland Park with a simple conviction: home remodeling shouldn’t be stressful, and homeowners shouldn’t have to referee between an architect and a builder. We brought design, engineering, and construction together under one roof so our clients get one team, one contract, and one point of accountability.

Over 17+ years, we’ve completed more than 1,500 projects across 20 North Shore communities — from kitchen and bath remodels to second-story additions and full whole-house renovations. Along the way we’ve earned 187 five-star reviews and an average rating of 4.9, built almost entirely on referrals from neighbors who’ve worked with us.

What We Believe

  • Price honestly, early. We engineer the budget during design, so the number you approve is the number you pay.
  • Respect the home. Many North Shore homes are decades old and full of character. We modernize them without erasing what makes them special.
  • Own the outcome. One team designs and builds your project, so there’s never a question of who’s responsible.
  • Communicate relentlessly. Weekly updates, clean job sites, and a project manager who answers the phone.
By the numbers: 17+ years in business • 1,500+ projects • 20 communities served • 4.9★ average rating across 187 reviews.

The Crestline Process

  1. Consultation — we learn your goals and discuss a realistic budget range.
  2. Design & engineering — plans, 3D renderings, and selections with live pricing.
  3. Fixed-price proposal — a detailed contract you approve before work begins.
  4. Construction — a dedicated PM, weekly updates, and meticulous craftsmanship.
  5. Walkthrough & warranty — we stand behind every project we complete.
